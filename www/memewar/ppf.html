Demand Psychedelic Freedom !!!
Support Psychedelic Pride !!!

Demand legalization and proper government regulation of cannabis, ecstasy, magic mushrooms, mescaline, LSD and other benign psychedelics !

  • Numerous evidence shows that benign psychedelics are indispensable in many spiritual and religious practices, and essential in artistic expression, philosophical and political discourse. These are activities protected by the First Amendment. Demand the repeal of anti-constitutional restrictions !

  • Benign psychedelics have many medical and psychological uses including treatment for depression, schizophrenia, obesity and anorexia. Hence their placement on Schedule I is fraudulent !

  • The genuine risks of benign psychedelics are far lower than the risks of alcohol, tobacco, driving and many other legal activities. At the same time, their spiritual and emotional benefits can be much greater. Demand rational and consistent government policy !

  • The main risks of benign psychedelics - persecution, fear, crime, and contamination by unknown substances - directly result from Prohibition, the government's failure to properly regulate psychedelic use and quality. Demand harm reduction !

  • The government abdicated its responsibility to provide protection to the people via control for quality of psychedelics and via regulation of their use. Such regulation could be done in a fashion similar to alcohol and tobacco regulation, or via a rational system of courses, competence tests, and self-prescription licenses. Demand that the government stops abdicating its responsibility !

  • There are widespread phobias against psychedelics and people who use them. These phobias are similar to racism, homophobia, anti-semitism, fear of poor people, etc. These phobias are largely due to the gross self-perpetuating misrepresentations in the media and in public discourse. Fight the phobias in others and in yourself ! Recognize and tell the truth!

  • Many people who use psychedelics feel guilty about it, because the society has labeled psychedelic use as "wrong". These guilty feelings are similar to those experienced by many Gays and Lesbians in a phenomenon known as internalized homophobia. The Gay and Lesbian community introduced the concept of Gay Pride in order to prevent this. If you use psychedelics, please recognize and support Psychedelic Pride, free yourself from your own internalized phobias ! There is nothing in benign use of psychedelics to feel guilty about ! Psychedelics can be used positively for personal and spiritual growth, creativity enhancement, better physical and mental health, and happier life. Just do not abuse them; do not endanger your and other people's lives, health, and careers ! If you use psychedelics, use them responsibly. Educate yourself about proper doses, intervals, techniques via books and the Web ! If you use psychedelics, consider whether your goals for such use are proper. Make sure that you can be proud of your psychedelic practices !!!

  • Stand for our unalienable Rights: Life, Liberty, and the Pursuit of Happiness !!!
