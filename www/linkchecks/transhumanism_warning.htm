Warnings are non-critical problems that should be looked into as part of the general maintenance of your site. Temporary redirects occur when a Browser requests a specific URL and the server redirects it to a different URL instead. Temporary redirects can slow down your site as the server must do more work than necessary to process the Browser's request. Permanent redirects occur when the URL requested by the Browser is either moved, deleted or is about to expire. Permanent redirects should be updated to the new location of the file.
