What does this Copyright mean?



I wrote my essays so that people could read them. I would not mind to get reimbursed for my work, so that I can devote more time and effort doing it, but I would never put any commercial considerations in the way of the distribution of my ideas.

So the copyright statement here is not too restrictive: Do not sell my work, keep it intact and mention me if you send it to somebody, etc. Also, I would request that you notify to me if you want to distribute my articles to a large number of people, translate them or do anything else significant.

You are welcome to create Web links to any of my essays. I would recommend that you link directly to my site, and do not copy the texts to your directories (unless you really have to). The reason here is that I keep updating my texts, and your copy may quickly become out-of-date.
